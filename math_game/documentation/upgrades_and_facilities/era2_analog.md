# Era 2: Analog Computing

====== NOTES ======

Mechanical marvels and probability engines. Gears grind out calculations. Steam and brass.

**Upgrade Structure per Facility:**
- 7 Tiered Upgrades: Unlock at 1, 5, 15, 35, 60, 100, 150 owned. Each doubles facility output.
- 2 Synergy Upgrades: Unlock at 15 of this facility + 15 of another. Grants % bonus per other facility owned.

**Price Multiplier:** 1.15× per facility purchase
